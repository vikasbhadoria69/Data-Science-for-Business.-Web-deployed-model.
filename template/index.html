About Data Science

Without the expertise of execs who turn cutting-edge technology into actionable insights, Big Data is nothing. Today, more and more organizations are opening up their doors to big data and unlocking its power—increasing the worth of a knowledge scientist who knows the way to tease actionable insights out of gigabytes of data.
Some advantages of data science in business:

  • Mitigating risk and fraud.
  • Delivering relevant products.
  • Personalized customer experiences.

It’s become a universal truth that modern businesses are awash with data. Last year, McKinsey estimated that big data initiatives in the US healthcare system “could account for $300 billion to $450 billion in reduced healthcare spending or 12 to 17 percent of the $2.6 trillion baselines in US healthcare costs”. On the other hand, though, bad data is estimated to be costing the US roughly $3.1 trillion a year.
